# Genre and Mood Mapping

## How Musical Genres Relate to Emotional States

Different genres carry cultural and acoustic associations that influence emotional response beyond just the raw audio features.

## Genre to Mood Mappings

**Pop**
- Moods: happy, energetic, euphoric, motivated
- Typical features: high valence, medium-high energy, 110–130 BPM
- Best for: mood lifting, social energy, motivation

**Lofi Hip-Hop**
- Moods: chill, focused, calm, nostalgic
- Typical features: medium-low energy, medium valence, 70–90 BPM, high acousticness
- Best for: studying, late night work, winding down

**Classical**
- Moods: melancholic, peaceful, focused, reflective
- Typical features: wide energy range, high acousticness, variable tempo
- Best for: deep focus, emotional processing, relaxation

**Rock**
- Moods: intense, angry, empowered, rebellious
- Typical features: high energy, medium-low valence, 120–160 BPM
- Best for: releasing frustration, physical energy, motivation

**Ambient**
- Moods: peaceful, meditative, spacious, calm
- Typical features: very low energy, medium valence, slow tempo, high acousticness
- Best for: meditation, sleep, background focus

**Jazz**
- Moods: relaxed, sophisticated, nostalgic, contemplative
- Typical features: medium energy, medium-high valence, 80–110 BPM
- Best for: creative work, social settings, unwinding

**Electronic / Synthwave**
- Moods: euphoric, focused, nostalgic, driven
- Typical features: high energy, variable valence, 110–140 BPM
- Best for: late night work, gaming, high-focus tasks

**Folk / Acoustic**
- Moods: peaceful, nostalgic, warm, grounded
- Typical features: low-medium energy, medium-high valence, high acousticness
- Best for: comfort, homesickness, gentle mood lift

**R&B**
- Moods: romantic, smooth, confident, emotional
- Typical features: medium energy, high valence, 80–100 BPM
- Best for: relaxation, romance, emotional connection

**Hip-Hop**
- Moods: energetic, confident, focused, hype
- Typical features: high energy, medium valence, 85–100 BPM
- Best for: motivation, confidence boost, physical tasks
